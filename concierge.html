Capabilities

What Axiom Concierge actually does for an agency.

It is not just messaging. It is a caseflow layer for intake, follow-up, coordination, evidence, reminders and controlled human takeover.

01 Two-way WhatsApp case inbox

Keep client messages linked to the right file, with quick replies, case notes and clear ownership.

02 Document intake by chat

Clients send documents on WhatsApp and the requirement is marked received against the case.

03 Voice notes into usable records

Turn voice notes into case notes, summaries and action prompts instead of leaving them trapped in chat.

04 Proactive follow-up engine

Run timed acknowledgement, missing-docs nudges, status checks and reactivation without spamming.

05 Appointment coordination

Propose a meeting, get confirm or decline, store the outcome, and trigger reminders before the event.

06 Human takeover protection

Pause automation when sentiment, legal risk, confusion or escalation signals say a person should step in.
